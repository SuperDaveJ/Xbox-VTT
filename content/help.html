When the Support Portal webpage has finished loading, you can interact with the model in the viewing window.

Click the Zoom In button to zoom in on the model.

Click the Zoom Out button to zoom out from the model.

When the Move Parts button is selected, you can click and drag parts to a different location.

When the Rotate Parts button is selected, you can click and drag parts to a different orientation.

When the Move World button is selected, you can click and drag anywhere on the viewing window to view the whole model from a different location.

When the Rotate World button is selected, you can click and drag anywhere on the viewing window to view the whole model from a different angle.

Click the Reattach All button to reattach all parts.
